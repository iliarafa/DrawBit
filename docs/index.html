WHAT IT IS

DrawBit is a focused pixel-art app for iPad. Pick a fixed grid, draw on it with the Apple Pencil or your finger, and export a sharp PNG.

HOW IT FEELS

  • Hard pixel edges at any zoom — nearest-neighbor everywhere
  • Two-finger pan, zoom, and rotate the canvas
  • Pencil, eraser, fill, eyedropper, and a swatch palette
  • Undo and redo as far back as the session goes
  • Export PNGs at 1×, 2×, 4×, 8×, or 16× scale

YOUR DATA

  • Everything saves locally on your iPad
  • No accounts, no cloud, no analytics, no ads
  • One-time purchase. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases
