COMMON QUESTIONS

Where are my drawings stored?

On your device. DrawBit does not use iCloud or any server. If you delete the app, your drawings are deleted with it. To keep a copy, export the PNG and save it where you'd like.

How do I export at a specific size?

Tap the share button. You can choose an export scale before generating the PNG.

Why does my exported image look pixelated when I open it?

That's intentional. DrawBit uses nearest-neighbor scaling so pixels stay sharp. Some viewers apply smoothing — try opening the PNG in another viewer or at 100% zoom.

Can I import an existing image to edit?

Not currently.

Does DrawBit support iPhone?

DrawBit is currently iPad-only.

Is there a subscription or in-app purchase?

No. DrawBit is a one-time purchase. There are no subscriptions, in-app purchases, or ads.
